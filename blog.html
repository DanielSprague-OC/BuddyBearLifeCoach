Rest, It's for Always

Rest is the most important one can do. It relieves all of the issues one may have. Too much work to do? Rest. You can't stress about work while resting. Ate too much food? Rest. You won't feel bloated while resting. If you aren't resting 24/7, then life isn't being done right. (Article written by Buster Blue.)

Take the Wheel of Your Life

The best thing I ever did in my life was take the wheel. Given, I am a dog, so most of that involves aggressively nudging people into giving me what I want, but the point still stands. Since taking the wheel, I have gotten at least twice the treats and petting sessions. There are no downsides. Only upsides. (Article written by Lulu Sprague.)
